// 01 — About

Who I Am

I'm Pedro Henrique Maciel, a backend developer based in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, focused on building well-structured, high-quality systems.

My main stack revolves around Java and Spring Boot, with solid experience designing REST APIs, relational databases with PostgreSQL, and scalable microservice-oriented architectures.

I care deeply about clean code, SOLID principles, and writing software that is easy to read, test, and maintain — not just software that works.

Currently expanding into DevOps, Docker, and cloud infrastructure to become a more complete backend engineer.
